Climate Education

Empower yourself and your community with climate science knowledge and actionable sustainability strategies.

Understanding climate change is the first step toward meaningful action. At Green Horizon, we provide accessible, science-backed educational resources designed for everyone—from curious beginners to experienced community organizers. Our materials emphasize hope, urgency, and practical solutions.
