About Green Horizon Foundation

Mobilizing communities to take meaningful climate action through accessible local projects, education, and policy advocacy.

Our Mission

Green Horizon Foundation believes lasting environmental change happens when people have the tools, knowledge, and support to act in their own communities. Since 2018, we've helped 247 communities launch 1,200+ climate projects, from urban gardens to solar co-ops to watershed restoration.

We're not waiting for perfect solutions. We're building them together—one neighborhood, one project, one inspired leader at a time.

Our Values

🌱

Hope in Action

Climate anxiety is real. We channel it into constructive projects that create visible progress and measurable impact.

🏘️

Local First

Global solutions start with neighborhood action. We support grassroots leaders and amplify community-driven change.

📚

Accessible Knowledge

Climate science shouldn't require a PhD. We translate complexity into clarity so everyone can engage meaningfully.

🔍

Transparent Progress

We share both successes and setbacks. Honesty builds trust and realistic expectations for lasting change.

⚖️

Justice-Centered

Climate solutions must address systemic inequities. We prioritize frontline communities most impacted by climate change.

How We Work

1

Listen & Learn

We start by understanding each community's unique challenges, assets, and climate priorities. There's no one-size-fits-all solution.

2

Build Capacity

Through training, mentorship, and resources, we equip local leaders with the skills and confidence to drive their own projects forward.

3

Take Action

Communities launch projects tailored to their needs—whether that's renewable energy, food systems, conservation, or climate advocacy.

4

Track & Share

We measure impact, celebrate wins, learn from challenges, and share insights to help other communities do the same.

Join the Movement

Whether you're an individual ready to get involved, a community organizer seeking support, or a partner aligned with our mission—there's a place for you at Green Horizon Foundation.
